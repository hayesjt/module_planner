About Module

Module Planner is a one-stop-shop for all of your planner needs. A friendly and simple interface allows users to be able to view the weather, create a to-do list, track their water goals, and maintain their budget all on one page. Our hope is that not having to click around and getting lost in an application will help users keep their goals more organized to improve lifestyle habits.
